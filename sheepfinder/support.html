常见问题

1. 关卡无法进入或显示锁定怎么办？

普通关卡需要按顺序通关解锁。完成前一关后，下一关会自动解锁。如果你购买了“整 App 买断”，请在商店页面点击“恢复购买”。

2. 金币没有到账怎么办？

请确认 App Store 购买已经完成。如果购买成功但金币未到账，请重新进入商店页面，等待商品状态刷新；仍有问题请联系我们，并提供购买时间、商品类型和设备型号。

3. 整 App 买断如何恢复？

进入游戏首页的商店页面，点击“恢复购买”。恢复购买主要适用于非消耗型商品，例如整 App 买断。金币包属于消耗型商品，Apple 通常不支持恢复。

4. 每日签到或每日挑战没有更新怎么办？

每日内容会根据设备日期计算。请确认设备日期和时区设置正确，然后重新打开 App。

5. 背景音乐或音效如何关闭？

进入设置页面，可以分别关闭“音效”和“背景音乐”。

FAQ

1. Why is a level locked?

Normal levels are unlocked in order. Complete the previous level to unlock the next one. If you purchased the full app unlock, open the Store page and tap Restore Purchases.

2. My coins did not arrive. What should I do?

Please make sure the App Store purchase was completed. If the purchase succeeded but coins were not delivered, reopen the Store page and wait for it to refresh. If the issue continues, contact us with the purchase time, product type, and device model.

3. How do I restore the full app unlock?

Open the Store page from the home screen and tap Restore Purchases. Restore purchases mainly applies to non-consumable products such as the full app unlock. Coin packs are consumable products and usually cannot be restored by Apple.

4. Daily check-in or daily challenge did not update.

Daily content is calculated from your device date. Please check your device date and time zone, then reopen the app.

5. How do I turn off music or sound effects?

Open Settings. Sound Effects and Background Music can be turned off separately.

2. 服务说明

2. The Service

本应用是一款单机汉字拆字解谜游戏。核心玩法是：玩家通过点选目标字的笔画，将其按预设方案拆分为偏旁部件。游戏所需的笔画与拆字数据均为本地资源，运行不依赖开发者的服务器。

The App is a single-player Chinese-character decomposition puzzle game. The core gameplay is: tap the strokes of a target character to split it into its radical components according to the predefined solution. All gameplay data is bundled locally — the App does not depend on the developer's servers to run.

应用功能可能包括但不限于：关卡挑战、笔画动画演示、提示与跳过、金币购买、关卡进度的本地与 iCloud 同步、分享卡片生成等。具体功能以应用最新版本为准。

Features may include but are not limited to: level challenges, stroke animation, hints and skips, in-app coin purchases, local + iCloud sync of progress, share card generation. Actual features depend on the latest version of the App.
